Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Arlaine Pastries

Ordering

You can place an order by filling out the contact form on our Contact page, sending us an email at arlainenywege41@gmail.com, or calling us on 0708 006 147. We will confirm your order and arrange delivery or pickup.

We recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance, especially for custom cakes. For large orders or special events, please contact us at least one week ahead to ensure availability.

Yes! We love making custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, and other special occasions. Get in touch via our Contact page and tell us your idea — we will work with you to bring it to life.

We accept M-Pesa and cash on delivery or pickup. Payment details will be shared once your order is confirmed.

Delivery

Yes, we offer delivery across Nairobi. Delivery fees vary depending on your location. Contact us to confirm availability in your area and get a delivery quote.

Yes, pickup is available. We will share the pickup location once your order is confirmed. Please do not arrive before the agreed time to ensure your order is freshly packed and ready.

Products

Yes, everything at Arlaine Pastries is baked fresh to order. We do not keep large quantities of pre-made products sitting around — your order is made with care when you place it.

We are working on expanding our range to include options for different dietary needs. Please contact us to discuss your specific requirements and we will do our best to accommodate you.
