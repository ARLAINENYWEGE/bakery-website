About Us

Get to know the story behind Arlaine Pastries

Our Story

Arlaine Pastries is a Nairobi-based bakery built around a simple idea: good cake and good pastries shouldn't be hard to find. What started as a personal goal has grown into a brand focused on quality baking, made fresh and made with care.

Right now, Arlaine Pastries focuses on cakes and pastries, with plans to introduce coffee and fresh juices as the business grows. Every product is made with attention to detail, using simple, honest ingredients.

Arlaine
