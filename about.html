About Us

Get to know the story behind Arlaine Pastries

Our Story

Arlaine Pastries is a Nairobi-based bakery built around a simple idea: good cake and good pastries shouldn't be hard to find.

What started as a personal passion has grown into a brand focused on quality baking — made fresh, made with care, and made with honest ingredients. Every product reflects attention to detail and a genuine love for the craft.

We currently focus on cakes and pastries, with plans to introduce coffee and fresh juices as the business grows. For now, we are committed to getting every bake right.

What We Stand For

Fresh Always

Everything at Arlaine Pastries is baked to order. We do not keep sitting products — your order is made fresh when you place it.

Honest Ingredients

We use simple, real ingredients in everything we bake. No artificial flavours, no shortcuts — just good baking done properly.

Made With Care

Every order gets our full attention. Whether it is one cupcake or a custom wedding cake, we treat every bake the same way.

Meet the Founder

Arlaine Nywege — Founder & Baker, Nairobi

Arlaine Pastries was started with a vision to bring quality, freshly baked cakes and pastries to Nairobi. The bakery was built on the belief that great baking should be accessible — not complicated, not overpriced, just honest and delicious.

Every product that leaves our kitchen is a reflection of that commitment. From the first order to the hundredth, the standard stays the same.
